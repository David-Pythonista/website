Photography

Of all forms of imaging, photography is the most closely related to reality. In the case of photographs, the authenticity comes from the technique itself, because the photograph is created by the rays reflected from the object. It has an important role in preserving and shaping individual and community memory. The photograph highlights, accentuates and attracts attention. The events of our individual lives and the history of society live more vividly in us and can be recalled more easily if a photograph has been taken of them. The photograph is suitable for recording reality, showing its manipulative distorted form, and can be art. [History and photography - Ilona Stemlern√© Balog]. I take pictures on different subjects, such as nature photos, landscapes, object photos, mood pictures or portraits.
